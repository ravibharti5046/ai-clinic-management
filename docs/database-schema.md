# Database Schema

## Main Tables

### users
Stores authentication and user roles.

### patients
Stores patient profile information.

### doctors
Stores doctor details and specialization.

### appointments
Stores appointment booking details.

### prescriptions
Stores doctor prescriptions and AI suggestions.

### bills
Stores invoice and payment details.

### medicines
Stores pharmacy inventory.

### lab_reports
Stores uploaded lab reports and AI summaries.

### ai_logs
Stores AI prompts and AI responses.

### followups
Stores patient follow-up information.

### notifications
Stores WhatsApp/SMS/email notifications.
